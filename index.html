Who We Are?

Waliv Global Services established in year 2011 and has been providing Maritime Administration and Fleet Services to International Shipping Companies and Ship Management Companies around the Globe. The concept of our services is unique, We are committed to provide complete freedom to Shipping Companies and ship Management Companies from their day to day admin work. We provide bespoke services to suit your company's requirements.

What we currently do?

We are currently providing Administration and Fleet Service to major oil/gas companies, handling over 50 ships and helping them reduce their admin burden both onshore and offshore.
